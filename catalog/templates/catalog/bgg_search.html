How to use:
  1. Text Search: Enter the name (or part of the name) of a board game
  2. Barcode Scan: Click the camera icon to scan a UPC/EAN barcode
  3. Click "Search Games" to find games on BoardGameGeek
  4. Select the game from the results
  5. Review price from multiple sources (EUR for Ireland)
  6. Add to your catalog!
Barcode Scanning Tips:
  • Hold phone steady, 15-30cm from barcode
  • Ensure good lighting or use flash button
  • Align barcode horizontally within the frame
  • Wait for the green scanning line to pass over barcode
Barcode Tips:
  • Look for the barcode on the bottom or back of the game box
  • Ensure good lighting and hold the camera steady
  • The barcode should be 8-13 digits (UPC/EAN format)
  • Try different angles if the first scan doesn't work
